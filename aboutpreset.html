INSTRUCTIONS FOR USING PRESETS

Hey there,

Thanks for getting the presets!

Here's how to use them in LR Mobile:

  1. First, get LR Mobile.
  2. Next, bring the DNG image into LR Mobile.
  3. You'll find it in LR Gallery, not your regular Camera Roll.
  4. Open the image.
  5. Tap the three dots at the top right, then hit "Create Preset."

To make your photos look best, adjust these three sliders:

  1. Temperature (for warmth)
  2. Exposure (for brightness)
  3. Contrast (for sharpness)

If you're stuck, there are lots of easy tutorials online. Or just shoot me a message, and I'll lend a hand.

Enjoy using the presets! Cheers!
