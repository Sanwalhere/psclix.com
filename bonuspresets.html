Instructions for Using Lightroom Presets

Thank you for downloading the presets!

Here's how to use them in Lightroom Mobile:

  1. First, download and install Lightroom Mobile.
  2. Import the DNG preset files into Lightroom Mobile.
  3. The presets will be in the Lightroom gallery, not your regular Camera Roll.
  4. Open a DNG image.
  5. Tap the three dots at the top right, then select "Create Preset."

For the best results, adjust these three sliders:

  1. Temperature (to adjust warmth)
  2. Exposure (to adjust brightness)
  3. Contrast (to adjust sharpness)

If you need help, there are many tutorials available online, or feel free to message me, and I'll be happy to assist.

Enjoy using the presets! Cheers!
